Unsigned app notice

Due to macOS security, unsigned apps are blocked by Gatekeeper. You can open IPMonitor in two ways:

Method 1: Open via right‑click (recommended)

  1. Find IPMonitor.app in Finder
  2. Right‑click (or Control + click)
  3. Choose "Open"
  4. In the dialog, click "Open" to confirm

After this, the app runs normally.

Method 2: Allow in System Settings

  1. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security
  2. Scroll down to the “IPMonitor” blocked message
  3. Click “Open Anyway”

Installation steps

  1. Click the button above to download IPMonitor.dmg
  2. Double-click the DMG, then drag IPMonitor.app to Applications
  3. Launch IPMonitor from Applications
